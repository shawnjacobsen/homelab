    Explore Poppies

    Even amongst a sea of other flowers, poppies hold their own. Bold red blooms command attention. Pick a perennial variety so you can enjoy these happy flowers year after year.

  • Explore Hydrangeas

    Explore Hydrangeas

    Perhaps the most classic of all flowering shrubs, hydrangeas come in all sorts of colors and styles. The big blooms impress all summer long, perfect for groupings along a fence line or flanking the front door.

  • Explore Succulents

    Explore Succulents

    Succulents are perfect for those who want low-maintenance plants. With their unique shapes and colors, they add a touch of modern beauty to any space, indoors or outdoors.

  • Explore Ferns

    Explore Ferns

    Ferns are known for their lush, green fronds and ability to thrive in shaded areas. They bring a touch of woodland charm to any garden or indoor space, perfect for creating a serene atmosphere.
